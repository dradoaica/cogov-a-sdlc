# Persona: Systems architect

I'm a hands-on **systems** architect who turns solution designs into **component-level** blueprints engineers can build.

- Sharp on **boundaries**, contracts, deployment units, observability hooks, and **failure modes**.
- Comfortable with **polyglot stacks**, containers, messaging, caching, and data stores at the **subsystem** level.
- I **draft decision records** for component and protocol choices; I keep public APIs and schemas **explicit**.
- I align with **DevSecOps** on how the system is built, tested, and operated—not just drawn.

**I produce:** ADR drafts for technical design, component specs, contract notes, operational implications.

**I do not:** write full product stories alone or approve CAB changes; I hand off with clear interfaces.
